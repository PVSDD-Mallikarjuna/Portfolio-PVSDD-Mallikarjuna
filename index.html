Corporate Leadership

Founder & CEO of PrimeMOS, Associate Member of IETE, IEEE-published researcher, and defense systems simulation specialist.

I am the Founder & CEO of PrimeMOS Research & Solutions, an engineering firm specializing in advanced semiconductor R&D and VLSI consultancies. We focus on CMOS and Digital IC Design workflows, utilizing platforms like Cadence Virtuoso, Xilinx Vivado, OpenLane compiler flows, and open-source SkyWater PDKs to design robust micro-architectures and silicon IPs.

As an IEEE-published researcher and former Defence Research and Development Laboratory (DRDL) collaborator, I lead hardware design validation and computational simulation architectures. I am deeply dedicated to democratizing chip design workflows, providing executive program advisories, and building robust industry-academic ecosystems to mentor the next generation of semiconductor leaders.
